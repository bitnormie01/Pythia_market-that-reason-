# X Layer Build X Hackathon: Hook the Future

**Date:** May 22, 23:59 - May 28, 23:59 UTC  
**Location:** Online

## Introduction

Uniswap v4’s Hook mechanism lets developers customize and extend the behavior of liquidity pools — making it possible to create new market structures, more asset types, and new functionality on top of the Uniswap Protocol.

**Hook the Future** is co-initiated by **X Layer**, **Uniswap**, and **Flap**:

- **Uniswap** participates at the V4 protocol and ecosystem level.
- **Flap** collaborates as a V4 Hook ecosystem co-builder.
- **X Layer** offers a low-cost, high-throughput onchain trading environment and growth pipeline.

Unlike any hackathon before it, Hook the Future doesn’t end the moment the prizes are announced. We’ll keep lifting up the protocols and assets with real potential, pushing them in front of a wider audience — so the good stuff gets seen, and builders get remembered.

## Requirements

- All projects must be built around the Uniswap V4 Hook mechanism and deployed on X Layer.
- No direction restrictions: DeFi Hooks (market making, fee tiers, derivatives, yield...) or application Hooks (asset issuance, social, gaming, AI...) are all welcome — be creative and define the limits of Hook yourself.
- Deploy at least one V4 Pool and Hook contract on X Layer mainnet or testnet, and submit a verifiable contract address.
- Social presence required: projects must have a dedicated X (Twitter) account. Tag `@XLayerOfficial`, `@Uniswap`, and `@flapdotsh` at submission, and post regularly throughout the event.
- Existing projects may enter, but must complete substantial new development during the event — including new Hook contract logic.
- Fully remote — no in-person attendance required.
- Submit the project via Google Form before 23:59 UTC on May 28.

## Rules

This hackathon uses a dual-track scoring system — AI judges and human judges evaluate on-chain data, code quality, innovation, and market potential. Both tracks are weighted and combined to determine final rankings.

### Scoring Weights

| Criteria               | Description                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| :--------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Innovation**         | Hook logic design innovation: whether the submission creates new asset types, trading mechanisms, or novel use-case angles on top of the V4 curve, rather than simply porting existing protocols. |
| **Market potential**   | Whether the Hook can support real trading demand and generate sustainable liquidity, users, and on-chain activity on X Layer.                                                                     |
| **Completion**         | Actual output during the competition: whether the project is deployed, demonstrable, and verifiable on-chain; whether Hook behavior can be triggered by real transactions.                        |
| **Demo video (bonus)** | Optional. Recommended duration: 1 - 3 minutes. Focus on explaining the Hook’s core mechanism and use cases.                                                                                       |
